Our Story

Founded on a Vision
of Inspired Living

Nyasa Haus was founded by Abhijeet Wankhade with a vision to bridge the gap between affordable, customised branded furniture and genuine design excellence.

The name Nyasa — Sanskrit for "foundation or placement" — reflects our belief that furniture is the foundation of every inspired space. Every piece we create is rooted in design, tradition, and functionality.
